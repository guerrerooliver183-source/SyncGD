# SyncGD
SyncGD is an advanced synchronization and protection mod for Geometry Dash. It ensures your progress is never lost by providing automated local and cloud saves.

## Key Features
- **Smart Auto-Save**: Automatically saves your game data at a configurable interval (default: 5 minutes).
- **Hybrid Storage**: Saves locally to `data.dat` first, then attempts a cloud sync to the Geometry Dash servers.
- **Offline Resilience**: If you lose internet connection, SyncGD keeps track of pending changes and synchronizes them instantly once you are back online.
- **Cross-Device Protection**: Detects new changes when switching between mobile and PC, ensuring you always play on your latest save.
- **SaveRetry Integration**: Automatically retries failed cloud saves with elegant on-screen notifications.
